This X Does Not Exist

Following its release, thispersondoesnotexist.com inspired a deluge of similar generators. A large portion of them are listed at the aptly named thisXdoesnotexist.com. The generators here feature varying levels of obscurity. On one end, you have the unsurprising thiscatdoesnotexist.com, compared to generators such as thiswaifudoesnotexist.com, which appeal to a much narrower subset of internet users. The generators are not limited to image data, with several having either text or audio capabilities.

After discovering this collection of generators, I had resolved myself to make my own unique contribution, thisfrogdoesnotexist.com. However, I was quickly heartbroken when I discovered that github user bitrate16 had already capitalized on this idea. Fortunately (or perhaps unfortunately for them), their site, thisfrogodoesnotexist.com is no longer online. With my renewed sense of purpose, I decided to continue on and attempt to reproduce thisfrogdoesnotexist.com.
